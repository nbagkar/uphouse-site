Frequently asked

How do I join a group?
UPHouse is invite-only. Ask a friend for their group's invite code, then open the app and choose Join a group to enter it.

How do I start my own group?
Create an account, then choose Create a group — you'll become the owner and get a shareable invite code.

Can I be in more than one group?
Yes. Use the group switcher on the Home screen to create, join, and jump between crews.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Edit Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and permanent.
